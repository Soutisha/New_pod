Here are 8 user stories that cover the requirements for the e-commerce platform development:

1. User Registration and Authentication
Story 1: User Registration
As a Customer, I want to be able to register for an account on the e-commerce platform, so that I can save my information and track my orders.

Acceptance Criteria:
- I can fill out a registration form with required information (name, email, password, etc.).
- I receive an email confirmation after successful registration.
- I can log in to my account using my email and password.

2. User Registration and Authentication
Story 2: User Authentication
As a Customer, I want to be able to log in to my account on the e-commerce platform, so that I can access my order history and track my current orders.

Acceptance Criteria:
- I can log in to my account using my email and password.
- I can view my order history and track my current orders.
- I can reset my password if I forget it.

3. High Availability and Performance
Story 3: Scalable Platform
As an Administrator, I want the e-commerce platform to be scalable and handle a large volume of traffic, so that it remains available and responsive during peak periods.

Acceptance Criteria:
- The platform can handle a large volume of concurrent users without significant performance degradation.
- The platform can scale up or down to meet changing demand.
- The platform remains available and responsive during peak periods.

4. Cloud-Ready Deployment
Story 4: Cloud Deployment
As a Developer, I want the e-commerce platform to be deployable to a cloud environment, so that it can be easily managed and scaled.

Acceptance Criteria:
- The platform can be deployed to a cloud environment (e.g. AWS, Azure, Google Cloud).
- The platform can be easily managed and scaled in the cloud environment.
- The platform meets cloud security and compliance requirements.

5. Vendor Company Profile and Relevant Experience
Story 5: Vendor Profile
As an Administrator, I want to be able to view the vendor's company profile and relevant experience, so that I can assess their ability to deliver the project.

Acceptance Criteria:
- The vendor's company profile is available for review.
- The vendor's relevant experience is available for review.
- The vendor's company profile and experience meet our project requirements.

6. System Architecture Design
Story 6: System Architecture
As a Developer, I want the e-commerce platform to have a well-designed system architecture, so that it is maintainable, scalable, and secure.

Acceptance Criteria:
- The system architecture is well-documented and easily understandable.
- The system architecture meets our project requirements.
- The system architecture is maintainable, scalable, and secure.

7. Integration with Third-Party Services
Story 7: Third-Party Integration
As a Developer, I want the e-commerce platform to integrate with third-party services (e.g. payment gateways, shipping providers), so that it can provide a seamless user experience.

Acceptance Criteria:
- The platform integrates with third-party services (e.g. payment gateways, shipping providers).
- The integration with third-party services is secure and reliable.
- The integration with third-party services meets our project requirements.

8. Integration with Inventory Management Systems
Story 8: Inventory Integration
As an Administrator, I want the e-commerce platform to integrate with our inventory management system, so that it can accurately track inventory levels and prevent stockouts.

Acceptance Criteria:
- The platform integrates with our inventory management system.
- The integration with the inventory management system is accurate and reliable.
- The integration with the inventory management system meets our project requirements.

These user stories cover the requirements for the e-commerce platform development, including user registration and authentication, high availability and performance, cloud-ready deployment, vendor company profile and relevant experience, system architecture design, integration with third-party services, and integration with inventory management systems.
